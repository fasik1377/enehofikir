Education and health

Eneho Fikir Conducts Health Education Session for Tesfaberhan School Students

Eneho Fikir, in collaboration with GIV Society and the Tesfaberhan School Administration, conducted a health education awareness session for Grade 12 students. The program was organized with the aim of promoting students’ awareness about important health issues that affect their well-being and academic performance.

The session was held on March 11, 2026, and was attended by more than 200 Grade 12 students. During the program, professionals delivered educational presentations on several important topics including mental wellness, non-communicable diseases, HIV, and sexually transmitted infections (STIs).

The awareness session was highly informative and served as an eye-opening experience for many students. It helped them better understand the importance of taking precautionary measures and adopting preventive practices to protect their health.

In addition, the program encouraged students to make responsible lifestyle choices, maintain their health, and remain focused on their academic goals. Improving students’ awareness on such health matters can also contribute to better school attendance and motivate them to aspire toward higher education.

Eneho Fikir continues to work in partnership with different organizations and schools to support youth development through educational and health awareness initiatives.

Teacher development

Eneho Fikir Provides Capacity Building Training for Adwaber School Teachers

Eneho Fikir has been supporting school teachers through different capacity-building and motivational initiatives for target schools. As part of this commitment to improving the quality of education, the organization conducted a special training program for teachers from Adwaber Pre-Primary, Primary, and Secondary Schools.

The training was organized for a total of 70 teachers and focused on important topics including modern pedagogy and English language teaching approaches. The content of the training was carefully tailored to match the teachers’ interests and the priorities of the school.

The program was delivered by well-trained professionals who shared practical knowledge and interactive teaching strategies that can be applied directly in the classroom. The training took place on February 28, 2026, at the school’s hall.

The session was highly motivating and provided teachers with new perspectives on how to improve the quality of education and enhance classroom participation among students. Teachers expressed their appreciation for the opportunity to strengthen their teaching skills and learn new methods that support active learning.

Eneho Fikir remains committed to supporting educators and promoting quality education through continuous professional development and collaboration with schools.

Scholarship program

Eneho Fikir Organizes Educational Visit to Ethiopian Airlines Aviation University for Merit Scholarship Students

It is worth noting that Eneho Fikir has been implementing a Merit Scholarship Support Program to support vulnerable yet outstanding secondary school students since May 2025.

As part of its ongoing activities, Eneho Fikir organized an educational visit to Ethiopian Airlines Aviation University for students supported under its Merit Scholarship Support Program. The educational visit was arranged in collaboration with Ethiopian Airlines Group and ET Foundation. Twenty-five high-performing students (15 female & 10 male) from 11 government secondary schools in Addis Ababa, along with three staff members from Eneho Fikir, participated in the visit held on 5th February 2026.

The visit was highly educational, inspiring, and well organized. The students were particularly impressed by:
• The modern training facilities and advanced aviation simulators
• The professionalism and hospitality of the instructors and staff
• The clear explanations provided about aviation careers and training programs
• The strong emphasis on safety, discipline, and technical excellence.

Many students expressed that the visit broadened their understanding of the aviation industry and motivated them to consider future careers in aviation. The interactive sessions and guided tour were especially engaging and informative.

At the end of the program, students shared their feedback, and lunch was served at the Airline’s cafeteria.

We appreciate the time and effort taken by the Airlines team to answer students’ questions patiently and provide detailed information about the Aviation University.

Overall, the visit was a valuable and memorable learning experience. We look forward to future collaboration opportunities and educational visits.

  • Eneho Fikir Team

Partnership and donation

Grace Ethiopian Evangelical Church Supports EHDO’s School Feeding Program with USD 4,500 Donation

Grace Ethiopian Evangelical Church from Nashville, Tennessee, has demonstrated its strong commitment to education and community well-being by supporting Eneho Fiker Humanitarian and Development Organization (EHDO)’s school feeding program.

Eneho Fiker Humanitarian and Development Organization (EHDO) continues to strengthen strategic partnerships with government, private, and non-government actors to expand support for vulnerable students and communities through impactful, grassroots initiatives.

Previously, representatives from Grace Ethiopian Evangelical Church (USA) visited Tesfaberhane Secondary School to observe EHDO’s ongoing school feeding program and related initiatives. During the visit, the delegation engaged with students, teachers, and kitchen staff, gaining firsthand insight into how EHDO’s programs directly address students’ basic needs and improve educational outcomes.

Following the visit, on January 21, 2026, members of the Church donated USD 4,500 to support EHDO’s school feeding program, benefiting vulnerable high school students at Tesfaberhane Secondary School. The contribution will be used to provide in-school meals, reinforcing student well-being and learning.

EHDO sincerely thanks Grace Ethiopian Evangelical Church for its generous support and meaningful partnership. This collaboration stands as a powerful example of shared commitment to society, education, dignity, and lasting community impact.

Student support

Supporting Outstanding Students Through Merit-Based Scholarship and Community Engagement

Eneho Fiker is committed to improving educational outcomes for students in targeted public high schools in Addis Ababa. Through close collaboration with school administrations, teachers, parents, and local authorities, the organization supports vulnerable yet outstanding students. Since June 2025, 30 selected students from 12 high schools have been receiving monthly financial assistance, educational materials, and psychosocial support to help them succeed academically and prepare for higher education.

As part of this initiative, Eneho Fiker organized a motivational and support event for Merit-Based Scholarship participants on 03/01/2026 at Misraqgoh Secondary School. The event celebrated Christmas with students and fostered connections with visitors from the USA and Canada. Each student received 2,000 ETB as a holiday gift, while visitors donated over 60,000 ETB and pledged continued support. The event featured interactive discussions, puzzle competitions, and sports activities, making it both inspiring and memorable for students and the Eneho Fiker family.
