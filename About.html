About US

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

EHDO envisions a world where humanity is pursued and poverty is alleviated through the integrated efforts of responsible citizens.

Mission

Transform the lives of marginalized children from disadvantaged communities, addressing educational, health, and socio-economic challenges through a holistic development approach.

Sustainability & Accountability

Ensure financial, social, and environmental resilience with transparency.

Program Implementation Strategies

  • Needs Assessment: Identify specific needs and priorities.
  • Stakeholder Involvement: Engage teachers, parents, students, and community.
  • Evidence-Based Practices: Use proven methods.
  • Data-Driven Decision Making: Monitor progress using data.
  • Sustainability: Plan for long-term impact.
  • Monitoring & Evaluation: Evaluate effectiveness thoroughly.

Theory of Change

If: EHDO maintains a stable social, economic, and political environment while strengthening policies and systems.

Then: Children, youth, women, and persons with disabilities will be empowered and have access to essential services.

Impact: Better lives, decreased poverty, enhanced resilience, better protection for women and children, and easier access to healthcare and education.

Geographic Presence

Headquartered in Addis Ababa with 14 schools under key intervention areas. Expansion planned to other regions.

Governance & Leadership

23 founding members. General Assembly is the highest authority. Executive Director and Board Chair lead operations.

Staffing & Volunteers

7 full-time staff and over 50 volunteers support project implementation.

Collaboration & Networking

EHDO collaborates with government offices and NGOs, including AA Education Bureau, Yeka Sub City administration, GIV Society, Matheous Wondu Cancer Society, SERVE Global, WISE, Link Ethiopia, Imagine1day, Hope Enterprises, FIDO, TOGETHER Ethiopia, Marriot Hotel, Ethiopian Airlines, Tikur Ambesa, Paulos Hospital, and Aster Volunteers.

Enehofikir is a non-profit organization founded in 2019 G.C. We transform the lives of vulnerable children, teachers, young people, and women through education, healthcare, livelihood support, and emergency aid programs.
