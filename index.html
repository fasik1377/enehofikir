About EHDO

Founded in 2019 G.C, EHDO strives to make a meaningful impact in the lives of vulnerable children, teachers, young people, and women.

Our mission is to transform the lives of marginalized children from disadvantaged communities by addressing educational, health, and socio-economic challenges through an integrated and holistic approach.

EHDO runs education, livelihood, and healthcare-focused projects across 13 schools (pre-primary, primary, and secondary) in Addis Ababa.

Currently, it is implementing 5 projects across 11 sub-cities in the capital, reaching thousands of beneficiaries each year.

EHDO has reached a total of over 32,901 beneficiaries in the last 6 years and achieved significant milestones:

Student drop-out and absenteeism decreased while participation increased through the school feeding program.

The school feeding program has supported ~300 students yearly since 2019, while also creating jobs for mothers.

Medical campaigns in partnership with GIV Society & hospitals improved the health of 5,083 students and communities.

Livelihood support programs empowered vulnerable women, children, and disabled people with entrepreneurial and business skills.

Targeted schools received materials and training to improve capacity, textbooks, and teaching aids.

Emergency support programs (clothing, meal sharing, public centers) sustained communities in Yeka Sub City.
