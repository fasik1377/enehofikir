The Heart Behind Our Work

Support Education

Helping children and young people access quality education empowers them to break the cycle of poverty.Education builds a brighter future for communities and ensures sustainable development through knowledge, confidence, and opportunities.

Health & Wellbeing

We create healthier communities through health programs, awareness campaigns, and access to healthcare. Our efforts include medical checkup and treatments through referial linkage with partners.

Empowering Communities

We work hand-in-hand with communities to build resilience and opportunities for all. By fostering local leadership, providing training, and supporting volunteers, we ensure long-term social and economic empowerment.

🌍 Our Impact

EHDO has reached a total of over 32,901 beneficiaries in the last 6 years and achieved significant milestones:

Student drop-out and absenteeism decreased while participation increased through the school feeding program. This initiative not only ensures daily nutrition but also boosts learning performance and attendance across all supported schools.

Read More

The school feeding program has supported ~300 students yearly since 2019, while also creating jobs for mothers. By empowering local women as food preparers and coordinators, the project helps strengthen community bonds and sustainable livelihoods.

Read More

Medical campaigns in partnership with GIV Society & hospitals improved the health of 5,083 students and communities. Services included free check-ups, hygiene awareness, vaccinations, and nutritional guidance for better overall wellbeing.

Read More

Livelihood support programs empowered vulnerable women, children, and disabled people with entrepreneurial and business skills. These initiatives have created sustainable income sources and improved social inclusion in marginalized communities.

Read More

Targeted schools received materials and training to improve capacity, textbooks, and teaching aids. Teachers were trained in modern methods to enhance classroom engagement and digital learning approaches.

Read More

Humanitarian support programs (clothing, meal sharing, public centers) sustained communities in Yeka Sub City. These relief activities provided rapid responses during crisis periods and promoted resilience among vulnerable groups.
